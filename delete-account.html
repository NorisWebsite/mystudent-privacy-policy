Account Deletion Request

Last updated: February 2026

The MyStudent app allows users to request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to Request Account Deletion

Currently, account deletion is handled manually. To request deletion of your MyStudent account, please email us using the details below.

  • Email: amulyaburusu@gmail.com
  • Subject: Request Account Deletion
  • Include the email or phone number used to register in the app

Data Deletion Details

Once your request is verified, we will delete:

  • User account information
  • Student profile data
  • Any personal data associated with the account

Data Retention

Some non-personal or legally required data may be retained for up to 30 days for compliance and security purposes, after which it is permanently deleted.
